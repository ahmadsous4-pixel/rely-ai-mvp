Set the default for all units. Override per unit anytime in the platform. Most users start on Approval Gated and switch to Autonomous within a week.
🤖
Autonomous
Agents act without asking · you get post-action logs
Best for developers who trust the system. Voice AI calls, inspection scheduling, briefs — all fire without you touching anything. Review logs at end of day.
✋
Approval Gated
Recommended for new users
✓
Agents prepare each action and show you exactly what they'll do. You approve with one tap. Perfect for getting comfortable — takes 1–2 weeks before most users go Autonomous.
👀
Observer
Read-only · agents flag, you decide
Relay AI surfaces blockers and recommendations. You act on them yourself. Good for teams that want to see the system working before committing to automated action.
